Week of Patching

This is the collab page between Agile and Voxelith for the Week of Patching 2026. Week of Patching is organized by Paloma Kop on Mastodon. To see everyone's entries, follow the #WeekOfPatching hashtag on Mastodon.

Vestiges
Our first patch of the week. Vestiges is an ambient track that has vibes of a deserted coastline. One where you could wander the shores for hours upon hours with not a soul in site.
Purlieu
Tonight we tried for a city themed patch that evokes the hustle and bustle of the city. You can also feel the dark and dingy bits of the underbelly.
Xylem
This track sounds like something a plant could hear in the woods after rain. The sun is coming out and some of the last drops are falling off of the trees.
Delve
In the deep underground there is a cave. The cave is teeming with life and activity. How far does the cave go? No one knows...
Nocturne
This patch brings back memories of looking up at the stars in the backyard. Almost a lullaby for the day as it gets put to rest.
